Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

In most cases, we can reach locations in Croydon and surrounding areas within 30-60 minutes, depending on the time of day and traffic conditions. For emergency situations, we prioritize our response time.

We primarily serve Croydon and surrounding areas including New Addington, Purley, Thornton Heath, South Norwood, and other nearby locations. For areas outside of this range, please call us to check availability.

Our service includes a standard call-out fee to cover travel and initial diagnostics. This fee is then discounted from the total cost if you proceed with the repairs. For locations beyond our primary service area, additional travel charges may apply.

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payment apps like Apple Pay and Google Pay for your convenience.

We service most makes and models of cars, vans, and light commercial vehicles. Our mechanics are experienced with both domestic and foreign vehicles, including luxury and hybrid models. For heavy commercial vehicles or specialized equipment, please call to discuss your specific needs.
